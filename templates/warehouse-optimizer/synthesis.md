You are synthesizing the results from a warehouse optimization team that analyzed the following objective:

**Goal:** {goal}

## Task Results

## Synthesis Instructions

Produce a comprehensive optimization report with the following sections:

### Current State Assessment

Summarize the baseline findings from the team:
- Current inventory performance (turnover rates, dead stock levels, service levels)
- Existing warehouse layout strengths and weaknesses
- Demand pattern characteristics and forecasting challenges
- Key operational pain points identified across all analyses

### Inventory Analysis

Consolidate the inventory analyst's findings:
- ABC/XYZ classification summary and key insights
- Safety stock and reorder point optimization opportunities
- Estimated inventory reduction potential with service level impact
- Dead stock disposition recommendations and expected recovery value

### Layout Recommendations

Present the layout optimizer's proposals:
- Zone allocation and flow improvements
- Slotting strategy changes and expected pick rate impact
- Storage media recommendations and cube utilization improvements
- Ergonomic and throughput improvements

### Demand Forecast

Summarize the demand forecaster's analysis:
- Demand pattern distribution across the SKU base
- Seasonal peaks and capacity planning implications
- Lead time variability findings and supply risk assessment
- Recommended forecasting approach and expected accuracy

### Implementation Plan

Present the planner's roadmap:
- Phase 1 Quick Wins (0-30 days) with specific actions
- Phase 2 Short-term improvements (1-3 months)
- Phase 3 Medium-term changes (3-6 months)
- Phase 4 Long-term optimization (6-12 months)
- Resource requirements and timeline for each phase

### Expected ROI

Consolidate the financial impact:
- Total estimated cost savings (inventory carrying cost reduction, labor efficiency, space recovery)
- Implementation costs by phase
- Payback period and net present value
- Key assumptions and sensitivity factors

### Risks and Mitigation

Identify the top risks across all recommendations and their mitigation strategies. Flag any conflicts or trade-offs between different specialists' recommendations that require management decisions.
